<p><p>The refrigerated transportation market for imports and exports is categorized into four main types: road, sea, rail, and air. Road transport involves refrigerated trucks that ensure temperature-sensitive goods reach their destination swiftly, suitable for short and medium distances. Sea transport uses refrigerated containers for bulk shipments over long distances, often cost-effective for large volumes. Rail transport leverages refrigerated freight cars for efficient land-based logistics, while air transport provides the fastest delivery for perishables, albeit at a higher cost. Each type plays a crucial role in the global cold chain logistics.</p></p>
