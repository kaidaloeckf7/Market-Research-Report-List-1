<p><p>Biological Safety Cabinets (BSCs) are essential equipment in laboratories for protecting users, environment, and samples from hazardous agents. The market can be categorized into three main types: Class I BSCs provide personnel and environmental protection by drawing air through a front opening while allowing safe exhaust. Class II BSCs offer a combination of personnel, product, and environmental protection, featuring a HEPA filter for airflow. Class III BSCs are completely sealed and provide the highest level of protection, ideal for handling highly pathogenic materials.</p></p>
