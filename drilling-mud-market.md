<p><p>The drilling mud market is segmented into three main types: oil-based fluids (OBF), water-based fluids (WBF), and others. OBFs, primarily composed of oils, offer superior lubrication and thermal stability, making them ideal for challenging drilling conditions. WBFs, characterized by their water content, are more environmentally friendly and cost-effective, widely used in conventional drilling. "Others" include synthetic and non-aqueous fluids, catering to specialized applications where unique properties are required for specific geological or operational challenges.</p></p>
