<p><p>Metal packaging materials for the food and beverage market primarily include tinplate, aluminum, and film laminated steel. Tinplate is renowned for its corrosion resistance and ability to be easily molded, making it ideal for cans. Aluminum offers lightweight properties, excellent barrier protection, and recyclability, suitable for a variety of beverages. Film laminated steel combines the strength of steel with a protective film layer, enhancing durability and resistance to contaminants, making it effective for packaging food products. Together, these materials ensure product integrity and safety.</p></p>
