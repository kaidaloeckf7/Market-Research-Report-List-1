<p><ul><li>Surface Detecting</li><li>Subsurface Flaws Detecting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive magnetic flaw detecting solutions encompass technologies aimed at identifying imperfections in materials used in vehicle manufacturing. Surface detecting methods focus on identifying flaws that are visible on or near the material's exterior, utilizing techniques such as magnetic particle inspection. In contrast, subsurface flaw detection targets issues located beneath the surface, employing advanced methods like eddy current testing and magneto-optic techniques. Both types are critical for ensuring the integrity, safety, and performance of automotive components.</p></p>
