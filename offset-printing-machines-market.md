<p><ul><li>Web Offset Printing Machines</li><li>Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offset printing machines market is divided into two main types: web offset printing machines and sheet-fed offset printing machines. Web offset printers utilize large rolls of paper, making them ideal for high-volume printing tasks like newspapers and magazines, as they allow for continuous printing. In contrast, sheet-fed offset printers operate with individual sheets of paper, providing superior image quality and flexibility, suitable for shorter runs such as brochures, packaging, and business cards.</p></p>
