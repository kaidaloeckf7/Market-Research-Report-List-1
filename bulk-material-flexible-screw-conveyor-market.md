Round screw conveyors are designed for efficient transportation of granular and powdered materials, utilizing a helical screw within a tube to minimize product degradation. In contrast, flat screw conveyors are optimized for specific applications, offering a broader surface area for enhanced stability, making them suitable for bulkier materials. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, ensuring efficient material handling and improving operational productivity.</p></p>
