<p><p>TEM specimen preparation equipment includes various tools essential for preparing samples for Transmission Electron Microscopy. The PIPS system (Precision Ion Polishing System) employs ion beams to thin specimens without damaging them. Dimple grinders create a rounded, thin film in a sample, while disc punch equipment cuts precise discs from materials for analysis. Disc grinders further refine surfaces for optical clarity. Other equipment encompasses ancillary tools like polishing machines and etchers, facilitating diverse preparation processes for high-quality TEM samples.</p></p>
