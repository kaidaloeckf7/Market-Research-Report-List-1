<p><p>Apple harvesters are categorized into two main market types: half-row and full-row harvesters. Half-row harvesters operate on a single row of trees, efficiently collecting apples while minimizing damage to the surrounding foliage. In contrast, full-row harvesters can harvest from two rows simultaneously, significantly increasing productivity and reducing labor costs. Both types vary in design and functionality, catering to different orchard sizes and operational preferences, ultimately enhancing the efficiency of apple harvesting processes to meet growing market demands.</p></p>
