<p><p>Cardiac biomarkers testing market includes several key types that help in diagnosing and managing cardiovascular conditions. Myocardial Muscle Creatine Kinase (CK-MB) indicates muscle damage. Troponins (T and I) are specific markers for myocardial injury. Myoglobin reflects early myocardial infarction. Brain Natriuretic Peptide (BNPs or NT-proBNP) assesses heart failure severity. Ischemia-modified Albumin (IMA) suggests myocardial ischemia. Other biomarkers may include various proteins and enzymes, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and patient outcomes in cardiovascular care.</p></p>
