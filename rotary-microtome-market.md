<p><p>The Rotary Microtome market is segmented into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual microtomes require skilled operators to control the slicing process, providing precision but demanding more effort. Semi-automatic microtomes offer a balance, with some automated features to enhance efficiency while still requiring operator input. Fully automatic microtomes maximize workflow efficiency by automating the entire sectioning process, minimizing manual intervention and ensuring consistent results, making them suitable for high-throughput laboratories.</p></p>
