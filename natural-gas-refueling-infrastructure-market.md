<p><p>The natural gas refueling infrastructure market primarily consists of Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) stations and Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) stations. CNG stations compress natural gas to high pressures, offering a cost-effective and efficient refueling option for vehicles. In contrast, LNG stations cool natural gas to a liquid state, allowing for greater storage density and suitability for heavy-duty transportation. Both types of stations support the growing demand for cleaner fuel alternatives and contribute to reducing greenhouse gas emissions in the transportation sector.</p></p>
