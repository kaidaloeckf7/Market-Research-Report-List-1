<p><ul><li>Corn Portion Cups</li><li>Sugarcane Portion Cups</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biodegradable portion cups market comprises primarily two types: corn portion cups and sugarcane portion cups. Corn portion cups are made from polylactic acid (PLA), derived from cornstarch, offering a sustainable alternative to plastic. Sugarcane portion cups, on the other hand, are crafted from bagasse, the fibrous byproduct of sugar extraction. Both types are designed to break down naturally, reducing environmental impact. They serve as eco-friendly options for food service and catering industries, promoting sustainability and waste reduction.</p></p>
