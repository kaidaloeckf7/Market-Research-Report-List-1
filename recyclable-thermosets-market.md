<p><strong>The Recyclable Thermosets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Recycling</li><li>Energy Recycling</li><li>Feedstock Recycling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recyclable thermosets market encompasses three primary recycling methods. Mechanical recycling involves physically grinding thermoset materials into smaller pieces for reuse in new products. Energy recycling captures the thermal energy released during the incineration of thermosets, converting it into usable energy. Feedstock recycling breaks down thermosets into their chemical components, allowing for the creation of new materials. Each method aims to reduce waste and enhance the sustainability of thermosets, facilitating a circular economy in polymer production.</p></p>
