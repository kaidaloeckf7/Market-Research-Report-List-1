<p><p>Business Process Management (BPM) Software is categorized into two main types: Cloud-based and On-Premise solutions. Cloud BPM software is hosted on the vendor’s servers, enabling easy access, scalability, and reduced IT costs, making it ideal for companies seeking flexibility and remote collaboration. In contrast, On-Premise BPM software is installed locally on a company’s own servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring more upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
