<p><p>The Marine Shock Absorber Market is primarily categorized into two types: elastomer and plastic. Elastomer shock absorbers utilize rubber-based materials to absorb energy and reduce vibrations, offering flexibility and durability in harsh marine environments. In contrast, plastic shock absorbers are lightweight and resistant to corrosion, making them suitable for specific applications where weight reduction is critical. Both types enhance vessel performance, safety, and comfort by mitigating shock forces experienced during navigation or rough sea conditions.</p></p>
