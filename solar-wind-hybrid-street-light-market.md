<p><p>The solar-wind hybrid street light market features two primary types of photovoltaic technologies: monocrystalline and polycrystalline. Monocrystalline panels are made from single-crystal silicon, offering higher efficiency and space efficiency, making them ideal for compact designs. In contrast, polycrystalline panels are composed of multiple silicon crystals, providing a cost-effective solution, albeit with slightly lower efficiency. Both types harness solar energy to complement wind power, enhancing energy generation for street lights while promoting sustainability in urban environments.</p></p>
