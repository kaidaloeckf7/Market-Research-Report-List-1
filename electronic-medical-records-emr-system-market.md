<p><p>The Electronic Medical Records (EMR) system market is primarily categorized into two types: cloud-based and web-based solutions. Cloud-based EMRs are hosted on remote servers, allowing healthcare providers to access patient records anytime, anywhere with an internet connection. This model enhances data security and reduces infrastructure costs. In contrast, web-based EMRs operate through a web browser, requiring less local hardware while enabling real-time collaboration. Both types improve efficiency, streamline workflows, and enhance patient care by providing easier access to health data.</p></p>
