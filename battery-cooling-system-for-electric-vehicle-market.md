In contrast, liquid cooling systems circulate coolant around the battery cells, offering superior thermal management, better performance, and improved longevity of the battery pack. As EV demand rises, selecting the appropriate cooling system becomes crucial for maintaining optimal battery performance and safety.</p></p>
