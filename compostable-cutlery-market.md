<p><p>The compostable cutlery market primarily includes bioplastics like PLA (polylactic acid), CPLA (crystalized PLA), and starch blends. PLA is derived from renewable resources like corn, offering a lightweight and eco-friendly option. CPLA, an engineered version of PLA, provides improved heat resistance, making it suitable for hot foods. Starch blends combine natural starch with other materials, enhancing biodegradability and performance. These alternatives cater to environmentally conscious consumers by reducing plastic waste and promoting sustainable practices in the food industry.</p></p>
