<p><p>The video borescope market includes two primary types: flexible and rigid borescopes. Flexible borescopes feature a malleable insertion tube that allows for navigation through tight or complex spaces, ideal for inspecting inaccessible areas in machinery and structures. Rigid borescopes, on the other hand, have a fixed, straight design, providing high-resolution imaging for direct line-of-sight inspections. Both types are essential in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and building maintenance, facilitating thorough examinations without the need for disassembly.</p></p>
