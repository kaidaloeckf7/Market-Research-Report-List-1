<p><strong>The MRI Motion Tracking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li> Marker Systems</li><li>Marker-less Systems </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI motion tracking system market is divided into two primary types: marker systems and marker-less systems. Marker systems employ physical markers placed on or near the subject to track their movements within the MRI environment. These markers are detected by sensors, ensuring precise monitoring during scans. In contrast, marker-less systems utilize advanced algorithms and computer vision techniques to track motion without physical markers, offering increased patient comfort and improved workflow efficiency in MRI processes while maintaining image quality.</p></p>
