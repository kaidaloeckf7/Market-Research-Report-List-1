<p><p>Refrigerated van trailers are essential in the logistics of perishable goods, categorized into two main types: frozen transport and chilled transport. Frozen transport maintains temperatures typically below -18°C, ideal for items like ice cream and frozen meats, ensuring long-term preservation. Chilled transport, on the other hand, operates between 0°C to +5°C, suitable for products such as dairy, fruits, and vegetables that require shorter-term cooling. Both types are crucial for maintaining food quality and safety during transportation.</p></p>
